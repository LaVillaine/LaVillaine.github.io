Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 400g minced beef
  • 2 scotch bonnet chillies (ghost pepper)
  • 3 sticks spring onion
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 150g mushrooms
  • 500g pasta sauce
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  •  

Tips

Serve on pasta with grated cheese and some garlic bread

Only wash the mushrooms right before they're about to enter the pot so that they don't become soggy

Method

  1. Chop mushrooms and keep in a separate bowl
  2. Stir fry minced beef in a pan for 2 - 3 minutes and cover in a separate bowl
  3. Chop tomatoes, chillies, spring onions and keep aside
  4. Chop onions and stir fry in hot oil until they start browning
  5. Add the chopped vegetables (except mushrooms) to the pot and sprinkle salt, oregano and basil
  6. Cook on low heat until veggies are soft and slightly brown, now add the mushrooms to the pot
  7. Cover on low heat for a further 3 minutes then pour in the pasta sauce and allow to simmer on medium heat for 5 minutes
  8. Add the minced beef and stir regularly for 5 - 10 minutes on medium heat
  9. Cover the pot on low heat for 5 more minutes before serving
