4 green plantains
500g oxtail
200g diced beef
200ml palm oil
mbonga[optional]
ghost pepper
1 chopped onion
4 garlic cloves
thumb sized ginger
1/2 stem of leek
2 tbsp ground njangsa
1 tbsp ground country spices (pebe[monodora myristica], country onion[afrostyrax lepidophyllus], essesse[Tetrapleura tetraptera], njangsa[Ricinodendron heudelotii], bongo [aframomum melegueta]

steps
peel and wash the plantains. cut into 6 pieces each and keep in a bowl of water
blend all the spices together (onion, pepper, garlic, ginger, leek, country spices]
heat the oil in a large pot. salt the meat and stir fry for 5 minutes 
add plantains, half the blended spices, 2 maggi crevette and half a litre of water.
Bring the mixture to the boil and cook on high heat for 20minutes
